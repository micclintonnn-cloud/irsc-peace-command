Established 2012

Upholding Dignity Through the Unity of None.

The Inter-Religious Solidarity Council is a Section 8 collective. We operate at the intersection of constitutional law and neighborhood-level peacekeeping to institutionalize peace in Mumbai.

Our Mission

To proactively mitigate communal friction by creating shared platforms where faith is a bridge, not a barrier. We act as a civilian buffer during times of crisis and a developmental partner during times of peace.

Our Vision

A Mumbai where diversity is treated as a strategic national asset, and where the constitutional rights of every citizen are protected by the collective will of every faith community.
